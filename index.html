About Us

We aim that no student is unaware of the opportunities around them, let it be open source events, hackathons, internships, etc. We also aim that, in this pandemic, every student learns some skills and is able to showcase them in their resume. Through this community, we want to reach each and every student.

But Why Us?

  • ● You will have access to tons of free resources.
  • ● Updates about events, hackathons, internships, etc.
  • ● We will be planning several webinars conducted by industry experts, to guide students on trending skills and latest technologies.
  • ● Community meetings will be held frequently to address the queries of the students.
  • ● You can learn new skills and add them to your resume.
  • ● You will get knowledge on how to build a strong profile on LinkedIn and Github (it's underestimated!).
Do we have to invest money?
No!!!! Just invest some time and that would be enough. Remember, it's a community by the students, for the students, and of the students.
